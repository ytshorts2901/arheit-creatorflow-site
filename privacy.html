Privacy Policy

Effective date: 15 July 2026

Arheit CreatorFlow is operated by Viktor Arheit in Switzerland. This policy explains how the desktop application handles information when an authorized creator connects a TikTok account and uploads a video as a draft.

Information handled

  • TikTok account identifiers made available through authorized scopes, such as open ID, display name, and avatar.
  • OAuth access and refresh tokens required to maintain the authorized connection.
  • Local video metadata, including filename, file size, duration, format, resolution, and cryptographic hash.
  • Draft-upload metadata, including upload time, publish ID, status, and technical error logs.
  • Support communications voluntarily sent by the user.

Purposes

Information is used only to authenticate the authorized creator, validate selected files, upload videos to the creator’s TikTok inbox as drafts, display upload status, protect the service, troubleshoot errors, and respond to support requests.

Video files

Videos remain local unless the creator explicitly confirms a draft upload. A confirmed upload sends the selected video to TikTok through TikTok’s official Content Posting API. TikTok processes the video under its own terms and privacy policy.

Sharing

Information is not sold. It is shared only with TikTok as necessary to provide the requested authorization and draft-upload functions, or when required by law.

Retention

OAuth tokens are retained until they expire, are revoked, or the user disconnects the account. Technical logs are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for security and troubleshooting. Local videos are controlled by the user and are not copied to an independent cloud storage service by Arheit CreatorFlow.

Security

The application uses OAuth 2.0, PKCE, state validation, restricted local storage, and encrypted token storage where supported. No method of storage or transmission is completely risk-free.

User choices

Users may revoke TikTok authorization through TikTok, disconnect the account within the application, delete local logs and tokens, and request assistance through the contact address below.

International processing

TikTok may process information in countries described in TikTok’s privacy documentation. The desktop application itself primarily processes local data on the user’s device.

Children

Arheit CreatorFlow is not intended for children and is available only to users permitted to operate the connected TikTok account.

Changes

This policy may be updated when the application or legal requirements change. The effective date will be revised when material changes are published.
